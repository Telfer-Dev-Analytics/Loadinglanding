Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of events do you host?

We offer a diverse range of events, from AI and coding workshops to thrilling case and pitch competitions. Our standout event last year was BTCC - the Business Technology Case Competition. What made BTCC unique was its focus on real-world tech scenarios, pushing participants to think differently. We partnered with three other schools nationwide, making it an incredible experience for everyone involved.

How will joining BTA benefit me?

Joining BTA means gaining access to exclusive opportunities that other clubs might not offer. While other clubs focus on traditional areas like marketing or finance, we specialise in preparing you for the rapidly evolving tech industry. Plus, technology is a game-changer for all fields—ever heard of fintech? That’s just one example of how tech and other disciplines go hand in hand.

Are there opportunities for leadership or involvement in projects?

Absolutely! We’re on the lookout for two first-year reps for the upcoming school year. As a first-year rep, you’ll dive into event planning and gain valuable experience working with our teams and understanding how our club operates behind the scenes.

Do you offer networking with industry professionals?

Every event we organise includes networking opportunities with top industry professionals. You’ll get the chance to meet and learn from experts from companies like IBM, Google, and Amazon. Don’t miss out on connecting with some of the biggest names in tech!

What are the membership fees and what do they cover?

Membership is just $10 for the whole year! This fee covers access to all our events throughout both semesters, including costs for event planning, prizes, and guest speakers.
